My Collection

A small but growing collection – each one with a story. Let's start from the left side !

  • Tissot PRX (Quartz) My first luxury watch – understated, integrated bracelet and punches way above it's weight. A gift from my father.
  • Casio A168 (Quartz) First watch I bought with my own money. lightweight and reliable. But arm hair gets in the way sometimes lol.
  • Seiko SRPD61K1 Desk Diver (Automatic) First automatic I purchased, one of my most complimented watches.
  • G-Shock GA2100 Casioak (Quartz) Rugged and reliable piece, follows the shape of the legendary Audemars Piguet Royal Oak.
  • Orient Bambino Small Seconds (Automatic) Finest dress watch I own, looks way more expensive than it actually is. Acquired this piece when I hit a financial milestone.
  • HMT Kohinoor (Quartz) India's first watch company HMT, known for their durable and reliable quartz pieces. Was gifted by someone special to me.
